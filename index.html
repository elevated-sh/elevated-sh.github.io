The process

If you’re based in London, UK, we sit with your team for a few days, and together we can decide the next steps. After the initial assessment, we become remote members of the team, and help you get to the next stage in your business.

There will always be an architect at the end of your Slack channel to bounce off a quick question, no matter which part of your stack it’s about.

We take care of security, so you don’t have to worry about breaches, then help you make it part of your day-to-day process.

When we’re done, and you’ve raised your next round, we help you hire someone to replace us.
